Hi, I'm Karan. I aim to do work that brings meaningful change. I currently work at Aryn, a document intelligence startup, where I've built and led the development of AI applications, worked with customers on integrating novel workflows, and trained and launched new architectures. I am particularly interested and experienced in multimodal AI, due to its wide ranging applications and positioning for the next phase of AI development.

I hold an M.S. in Data Science and B.S.E in Networked and Social Systems, both from the University of Pennsylvania. During my time there, I served as a teaching assistant to Zachary Ives and Swapneel Sheth, and conducted research with Duncan Watts.

Before that, I completed my high school at a boarding school in the foothills of the Himalayas. Here I learnt the value of resilience, fortitude, and self-belief. During this time I also represented India for debate, as part of a team of 5 that was selected from over 8000.

In my free time, I like to read, make and watch movies, and go on hikes. To reach out, email me at me@karansampath.com.
