National Park Ranking

Tier 1: The Crown Jewels ⭐⭐⭐

  1. Glacier National Park, MT: You can spend months in the stunning beauty of the lakes, glaciers, and animals here.

  2. Yosemite National Park, CA: Hikes, views and valleys. I only wish it wasn’t so touristed (though I guess I’m part of the problem).

  3. Kenai Fjords National Park, AK: Hiked on a glacier, 10/10 experience.

Tier 2: Exceptional Destinations ⭐⭐

  1. Acadia National Park, ME: Sunrise here was an incredible experience.

  2. Crater Lake National Park, OR: Come for how blue the water is, it will leave you spellbound.

  3. Denali National Park, AK: Saw this in shoulder season. A stunning sight with snow all over.

Tier 3: Worthwhile Visits ⭐

  1. Sequoia & Kings Canyon National Parks, CA: Being in the presence of these trees made me feel spiritual.

  2. Pinnacles National Park, CA: The main hike, even if singular, is well worth it for the condors on the way.

  3. Shenandoah National Park, VA: Great place for rock scrambling.
